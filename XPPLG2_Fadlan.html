A dentist made a game in MS Paint and it's terrifying

'World of Horror' is what happens when you mix Junji Ito with HP Lovecraft.

The scariest things about World of Honor might be the fact that it's being lovingly crafted by a part-time dentist. Pawel Konzmiski is the sole creator of world of honor, and he also practices dentistry in poland, splitting his time betwen poking at people's mollars and placing pixels in the perfect spots using MS paint. Yes, that paint.

World of Honor is borne of strange circumstances, which might explain why the game it self is so unsettling. It plays out in five short stories set in a seaside village in 1980s Japan, whose residents are falling into mass madness while eldritch monster rises form the seas. Earie chiptune music plays trough out the game, farntic and propelling. People have gone missing, fastering demons are walking the streets, and sushi chef's are serving Omakase with human eye balls on the platter. Fight scenes are turn-based, forcing you to stare down your fears and face slaughter one move at a time.
